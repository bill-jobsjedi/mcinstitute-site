Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

MC Education Services LLC, doing business as MC Institute ("MC Institute," "we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

MC Institute is an English-language school and international student services center based in Orlando, Florida. For any privacy-related question you may contact us at contact@mcinstituteisc.com or +1 407-421-0417.

2. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, phone number, mailing address, date of birth, country of citizenship, passport, SEVIS ID and Form I-20 information, academic history, and payment details when you inquire, apply, enroll, or contact us.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser and device type, and pages visited, through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Consent records: when you opt in to communications (including SMS), we record the consent text, date, time, and IP address of your opt-in.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process inquiries, applications, and enrollment, and issue and maintain Form I-20 and related SEVIS records.
  • Communicate with you about your application, classes, schedules, documents, appointments, and payments.
  • Provide customer service and respond to your requests.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including reporting to SEVP/DHS for F-1 students.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.

4. SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS text messages from MC Institute regarding your application and enrollment, including application status updates, document requests, appointment and start-date reminders, and customer-service follow-ups.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and reply HELP for assistance.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties. Service providers that help us deliver messaging may process the data solely to provide that service and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect site functionality.

6. How We Share Information

  • Service providers (for example payment processors, hosting, CRM, and messaging providers) that perform services on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Government and educational authorities as required by law, including SEVP/DHS reporting for F-1 student status.
  • Legal compliance when required to comply with law, enforce our agreements, or protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal information. As stated in Section 4, we do not share mobile opt-in or SMS consent data with third parties for marketing.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide our services and to comply with legal, accounting, and regulatory requirements (including records we are required to keep for SEVP/DHS), after which it is deleted or anonymized.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Student Education Records

Student records are handled consistent with applicable law and school policy. For F-1 students, certain information is shared with SEVP/DHS as required to maintain student status.

10. International Users

MC Institute is located in the United States and your information is processed in the U.S. By using our services from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years or older, or for minors with the consent and involvement of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Your Choices and Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and you may opt out of marketing or SMS communications at any time (reply STOP for SMS). To exercise these choices, contact us at contact@mcinstituteisc.com. We will respond consistent with applicable law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be posted on this page.
